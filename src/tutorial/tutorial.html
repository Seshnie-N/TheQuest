How to Play:

The aim of the game is to explore the maize, fight monsters and get out!

During the game, you will come across gates. These gates prevent you from getting out.

To unlock the gates, you must get keys. But beware, for this is no easy task! Monsters hold these keys and must be slayin to obtain them.

Goodluck on your Quest and do not DIE!

Controls:

Use the "WASD" keys to move

Use "Space-bar" to jump

Use "Shift" to run

click the monster to hit it

In order to complete a level, place the key in the gate to open up the next section of the maize.
